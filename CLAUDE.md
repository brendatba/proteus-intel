# Proteus Intel

## What This Project Does
Website for Proteus Intel — a sovereign infrastructure and defense enablement platform that structures, aligns, and activates capital, capability, and trusted access networks across allied and partner-nation defense markets. Operates at the intersection of sovereign wealth, emerging defense technology, Web3 infrastructure, energy sovereignty, and geopolitical intelligence.

## Brand & Design
- **Aesthetic**: Dark, high-end, intelligence-grade — not corporate SaaS
- **Color palette**: Gold (#C9A84C) on near-black (#080808), with layered dark surfaces
- **Typography**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), DM Mono (monospace), Barlow Condensed (sans-serif)
- **Tone**: Authoritative, precise, sovereign. Not salesy. The site should feel like a classified briefing, not a pitch deck.

## Five Verticals
1. Sovereign Capital Architecture & Family Office Access
2. Defense Technology Acceleration & IP Commercialization
3. International Web3 Sovereign Data & Power Infrastructure
4. Energy Sovereignty & Resilient Infrastructure
5. IP Services, Advanced Technical Supply Chains & Specialized Vertical Access

## Tech Stack
- Static HTML/CSS/JS (single-page site)
- Deployed on Vercel
- No build tools or frameworks currently — vanilla web
